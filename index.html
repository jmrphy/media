About

This site organizes the lecture notes for my 2017 class on the politics of media, information, and technology at the University of Southampton.

My homepage is jmrphy.net. You can also find me on Twitter, Youtube, Tumblr, and a few other places. I put out a podcast called Other Life and a newsletter of private letters, new work, and updates on my projects.

The site was built with Jekyll using the Tuft theme. The two-column design was inspired by the work of Edward Tufte.
